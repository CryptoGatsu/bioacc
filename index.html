What is Bio/acc?

Bio/acc stands for biological accelerationism. It is the belief that humanity should aggressively accelerate progress in biology and biotechnology. Rather than slowing innovation through excessive regulation, bio/acc advocates:

  • rapid biotech experimentation
  • AI driven biological discovery
  • open scientific collaboration
  • decentralized research funding

Crypto provides the funding rails. AI provides the discovery engine. Biology is the frontier.
